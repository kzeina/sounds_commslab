Faiza the Falcon: Around the World

THE STORY

NYU Abu Dhabi went quiet overnight. Students left, scattered across the world, and everything moved online. Faiza stayed for a while… then decided not to. She followed them. Not to bring them back—but to see where they had gone. Different skies, different homes, different lives—yet somehow still connected. When she returned, campus was still the same. But it didn’t feel as empty anymore.
